
- 1 token is approx 4 characters or 3/4 of a word
- 30 tokens is approx 1-2 sentences
- 100 tokens is approx 1 paragraph
- 1024 tokens is approx 750 words or 10 paragraphs
- 2048 tokens is approx 1500 words
